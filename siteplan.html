TrailBlaze Hiking Co. – Website Plan

Site Name

TrailBlaze Hiking Co.

The name “TrailBlaze” represents adventure, exploration, and leadership in outdoor hiking experiences. It reflects the company’s mission to guide hikers through exciting and scenic trails.

Optional domain: trailblazehiking.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about guided hiking tours, trail recommendations, safety tips, and booking options. It will serve as a hub for outdoor enthusiasts looking for organized hiking adventures and educational resources.

Scenarios

  • What hiking tours are available for beginners?
  • How can I book a guided hiking trip?
  • What gear should I bring for a mountain hike?

Color Scheme

  • Forest Green (#2E6F57) – Used for headings and navigation background.
  • Sand Beige (#F4EBD0) – Used for page background.
  • Charcoal Gray (#333333) – Used for body text.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings.
  • Open Sans – Used for body text.
