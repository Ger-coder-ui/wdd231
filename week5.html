Website Plan for Bay Area eBiking Club

Site Name

The site name is "Bay Area eBiking Club". This name was selected to reflect the region (San Francisco Bay Area) and the focus on electric biking (eBiking). It communicates clearly the purpose of the site.

Optional domain availability: bay-ebike.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to serve as a central hub for regional e-biking enthusiasts. It will feature e-bike trails, recommendations, and provide membership information for those interested in joining the club.

Scenarios

  • What is the best e-bike to get for mountainous areas?
  • Where can I find contact information for the club's directors?

Color Schema

Here are the two primary colors that will be used in the design:

Color #2a3d66 will be used for headings, navigation, and important buttons. Color #ffcc00 will be used for accents like call-to-action buttons and highlights.

Typography

The fonts selected for this project are:

  • Headings: 'Arial', sans-serif (bold)
  • Body text: 'Arial', sans-serif (regular)

The font family 'Arial' is chosen for its readability and web-safe compatibility.
